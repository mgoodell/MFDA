Summary

Welcome to the Registration Page for the 2023 Compliance Credit webcast, the second of 2 mandatory compliance credits for the first cycle of the Mutual Fund Dealer Continuing Education (“CE”) Program (previously the MFDA CE Program). The 2023 Compliance Credit provides guidance on the following topics:
 

Agenda
 
  • Updates: New Self-Regulatory Organization of Canada (“New SRO”) and CE - Brett Konyu, Director, Member Education and Membership Services
    Brett discusses the impact of the New SRO Interim Rules on Approved Persons and provides guidance on how CE Program Participants (Approved Persons) can satisfy their CE requirements by the end of the first CE cycle on November 30, 2023.
     
  • Know-Your-Client (KYC) Information - Cheryl Clark, Member Education Consultant
    Cheryl provides guidance to Approved Persons regarding KYC requirements, building from the 2022 MFDA Compliance Credit with a focus on determining risk profile and the impact of Client Focused Reforms.
     
  • Outside Activities - Iris Titov, Member Education Consultant
    Iris explains the recent amendments to National Instrument 33-109, 31-103 and related guidance including MFDA Bulletin #0925-P, and reviews the requirements on outside activities that are most relevant to Approved Persons.
     
  • Enforcement Guidance - Alan Melamud, Senior Enforcement Counsel
    Alan addresses prohibitions on Approved Persons acting as an estate trustee, conflicts of interest arising from being named a beneficiary of a client’s will, and conflicts of interest arising from sales incentives. In addition, Alan discusses MSN-0016 Electronic Signatures, including considerations for Approved Persons when making use of electronic signatures to comply with Interim Mutual Fund Dealer Rules, and client confidentiality.

     
Obtaining Credits
 

This webcast is the second of the two mandatory Compliance Credits under the Mutual Fund Dealer CE Program for the 2022-2023 Cycle. The first Compliance Credit, the 2022 MFDA Compliance Credit was issued in 2022 and is available for viewing in English and French by logging into CE Reporting and Tracking System (CERTS) and selecting “Register for MFDA CE Activities” from the “Actions” Menu. If you cannot recall if you have obtained the 2022 MFDA Compliance Credit, check under the CE details page/tab for completed activities. Credits for this webcast cannot be used towards the 2023 – 2025 CE cycle.

The number of credits you have taken will be automatically updated on CERTS the month after you viewed the entire 2022 MFDA Compliance Credit webcast or 2023 Compliance Credit webcast. To obtain these credits, CE Program Participants must register using their Participant ID # and view the entire webcast. Your Participant ID # pre-populates on this registration page if you have used the link within CERTS or in an email notifying you that the Compliance Credit is available.

Please note you must subscribe to ePublications Education Notices to keep informed of upcoming webcasts.
